PRODUCT PAGE RULES

  • Terms and Conditions

The following rules and restrictions apply to sellers who use the Fashion Add a Product feature:

  1. Using this feature for any purpose other than creating product details pages is prohibited.
  2. The use of false product identification information, including ISBN or EAN numbers is prohibited.
  3. Creating a product detail page for a product already in the Fashion catalogue is prohibited.
  4. Add a Product is to be used only to create pages for products that do not already exist within Fashion's catalogue.
  5. Using Add a Product to cross-merchandise or cross-promote a product is prohibited.
